About Us

RCK CORPORATION was founded in 2022 in Rancaekek, Bandung by four friends who share the same vision and passion in the world of fashion: Farhan, Novan, Maranatha and MAYLIA.
The name RCK itself was created because it is an abbreviation of Rancaekek (RCK).
Initially, RCK CORPORATION only sold plain t-shirts and premium jackets through social media and local bazaars. However, thanks to its simple yet strong design, as well as the unquestionable quality of the materials, this brand began to find a place in the hearts of young Indonesian customers.
